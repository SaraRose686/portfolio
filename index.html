A Little About Me

Born/raised in Maryland, I transplanted to California for college. Originally a pure mathematician, I fell in love with coding during my “Introduction to Programming” course. Prepared with additional computer science courses, I started my career as a Java Developer at Accenture after graduating with a B.S. in Mathematics. After a period working in management, I have returned to my original passion: development.

Today, I am fine tuning my web development skills in the Bloc Web Developer Track program. I am currently versed in HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Java, Spring, JSP, SQL, XML, C/C++, C#; as well, I have experience using Eclipse, Visual Studio Code, Git, GitHub, Bitbucket, Jira, Rational ClearCase, Oracle 11g, SOAP. Proficient with both Windows and OSX, I recently began using Ubuntu to learn more about Linux. In the near future, I also plan to explore the following topics: AJAX, APIs, React, Express, Jest, Enzyme, Zeit, Node, Postgres, REST/RESTful, Heroku, Angular, MongoDB, Python.
